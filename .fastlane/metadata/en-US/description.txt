Search for images from multiple online image collections!

Currently supporting:

● New York Public Library
● Library of Congress
● California Digital Library
● Digital Public Library of America
● Europeana
● LIFE Magazine photo archive
● National Library of Australia (Trove) 
● Flickr: Commons Project
● Flickr: Boston Public Library
● Flickr: Cambridge Historical Society
● Reddit: /r/HistoryPorn
● Reddit: /r/OldSchoolCool
● Reddit: /r/TheWayWeWere

OpenPics is open source.  

Wish it had more features?  Download the source code, and add it!   I'll update the app as new features are added.
